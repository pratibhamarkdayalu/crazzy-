SIMPLE RECIPES MADE FOR real, actual, everyday life.

cake

  • Ingredients:

    • 1 cup all-purpose flour 1 cup sugar
    ½ cup butter (soft)
    2 eggs
    ½ cup milk
    1 tsp baking powder
    1 tsp vanilla essence

  • Method:

    • Mix butter and sugar until fluffy. Add eggs one by one, then vanilla. Sift flour with baking powder and fold into mixture. Add milk gradually, mix well to make smooth batter. Pour into greased tin and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–35 minutes.

  • 250–350 calories

pizz

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    1 tsp yeast
    ½ cup warm water
    1 tbsp olive oil
    Salt to taste
    ½ cup pizza sauce
    1 cup mozzarella cheese
    Veggies (capsicum, onion, tomato, corn, etc.)

  • Method:

    • Make dough with flour, yeast, water, oil, and salt. Rest 1 hr.
    Roll out dough, spread pizza sauce.
    Add veggies + cheese on top.
    Bake at 200°C for 15–20 mins.

  • 250–300 calories

pancake

  • Ingredients

    • 1 cup all-purpose flour
    1 tbsp sugar
    1 tsp baking powder
    1 cup milk
    1 egg
    1 tbsp butter (melted)
    Pinch of salt

  • Method

    • Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
    Add milk, egg, and melted butter, whisk smooth.
    Heat a pan, pour a ladle of batter.
    Cook till bubbles appear, flip, and cook golden.

  • 90–120 calories
